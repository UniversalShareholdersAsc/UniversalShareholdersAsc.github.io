About Us

We are a non-profit organization that empowers anyone to become a successful business owner by providing hands-on training and support in all aspects of ownership and operation, enabling active participation in our entire economic system.

Businesses should be part of their communities with everyone who contributes significant value to them having an ownership share. We are committed to helping everyone achieve financial stability, work-life balance, and taking back ownership of our communities.

As participants progress through our program, they will gain equity Shareholder ownership in the businesses they have helped build.
